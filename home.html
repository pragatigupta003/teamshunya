Vision

"We are aspiring to build technological solutions for the construction of net-zero housing and provide quality living spaces built sustainably on an economically feasible budget."

About us

Team SHUNYA is a student-run organisation with a vision for a sustainable future. We are striving toward creating technologies and practices necessary for the sustainable construction of living spaces. Our team is a diverse group of individuals from varying technical fields perfect for the multidisciplinary approach required for our aim.

As the world moves toward carbon neutrality, Team SHUNYA will be a part of this journey and aspires to be a part of the solution to the complex problem of climate change faced by humanity.
