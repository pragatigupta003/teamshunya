ABOUT US

Team SHUNYA is a student-run organisation with a vision for a sustainable future. We are striving toward creating technologies and practices necessary for the sustainable construction of living spaces. Our team is a diverse group of individuals from varying technical fields perfect for the multidisciplinary approach required for our aim. As the world moves toward carbon neutrality, Team SHUNYA will be a part of this journey and aspires to be a part of the solution to the complex problem of climate change faced by humanity.


OUR VISION

We are aspiring to build technological solutions for the construction of net-zero housing and provide quality living spaces built sustainably on an economically feasible budget.


INTERNATIONAL COMPETITIONS

We are the only team representing India in the prestigious competition, Solar Decathlon, often referred as the ‘Olympics of Sustainable Architecture’ which is organised by US Dept of Energy.


  • SOLAR DECATHLON (PROJECT DAKSH)

    • We participated in SOLAR DECATHLON Design Challenge US, Colorado 2021 and bagged 2nd prize for the PROJECT DAKSH in the attached housing division and also received the graphic designer award for spectacular virtual background.


  • SOLAR DECATHLON (PROJECT H-NAUGHT)

    • We took part in Solar Decathlon at Versailles, France in 2014 with project ‘H-naught’ and we got "Honourable mention in Sustainability".


  • SOLAR DECATHLON (PROJECT SOLARISE)

    • We took part in Solar Decathlon at Dezhou, China in 2018 with ‘Project Solarise’. We were given the "Best Participation" award for completing the house in 12 days.
